Pancake Recipe

Here is a savory pancake recipe that's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 big eggs
  • Water
  • A pinch of salt
  • 10 Strawberries
  • Butter
  • honey

Directions on how to make Pancake

  1. Mix your flour, sugar, salt, evenly in a bowl
  2. Add water to a make semi thick paste.
  3. place non stick frying pan on the heat and add a little butter to grease the pan
  4. pour the paste in the pan and spread evenly to cover the pan
  5. flip the pancake upside down so that the other side can fry
  6. Place the fried pancake on the plate and garnish it with Strawberriesn and honey
