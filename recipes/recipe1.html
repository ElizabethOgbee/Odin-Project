Fried Fish and Plantain

Here is a savory fried fish and plantain recipe that's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 big Tilapia fish
  • 2 plantains
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • black pepper to taste
  • Â¼ large baguette, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste

Directions on how to make fried fish and plantain

  1. Preheat a frying pan to high heat.
  2. Mix together butter, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Spread the butter mixture generously on the Fish.
  4. Place slices on the preheated grill and cook until butter is melted and bread is lightly brown, about 30 seconds.
  5. Sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately.
