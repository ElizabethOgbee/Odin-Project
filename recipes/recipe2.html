Beans and Plantain Recipe

Here is a savory beans and plantain recipe that's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Brown beans
  • 2 plantains
  • 1/2 cup of Palm oil
  • 2 seasoned cube spice
  • 2 tablespoons of salt
  • 5 fresh red ball pepper
  • 2 big onions
  • Water

Directions on how to make beans and plantain.

  1. water and boil beans for 0ne hour to make it very soft and tender and a table spoon of salt.
  2. Grind pepper and onions, pour in a pot, place on the fire, add oil and fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Add salt, seasoned cube and stir.
  4. peel plantains and slice it.
  5. Add salt and place a frying pan on the fire.
  6. fry the plantain for 4 minutes
  7. Serve beans, sauce and plantain in a plate.
